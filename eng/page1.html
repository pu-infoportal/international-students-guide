About Us

Meet the team and our design concept

International students usually face various challenges.
We hope this website can help them solve problems in different aspects of daily life. 

Motivation

We hope to help international students who come to Providence University, whether for exchange programs, university studies, or learning Chinese. We aim to let them become more familiar with the school and its surroundings, while also making it easier for them to find the necessary information for daily life. 

Features

To provide an information portal, which includes content about life at Providence University (covering on-campus facilities, restaurants, and dormitories), nearby food spots, transportation in Taichung, as well as recommendations for leisure activities and attractions. 

Target Users

Our target users include newly arrived international students at Providence University, as well as any international students who need it. We want to provide user-friendly information on the website to anyone who may be curious about information on campus life, food, transportation, and entertainment. 

Context of Use

International students tend to be curious about new surroundings and cultures, but they might also feel unsettled or anxious. With this information portal, they can use it wherever and whenever they need, which will assist them in adjusting and integrating more quickly into life in Taiwan.
