Healthcare

Source: Office of Student Affairs - Counseling and Health Center

Are you feeling ill or have suffered an injury or accident?

The school nurse will perform a professional assessment and provide nursing care, monitor the patient's condition, or assist students in transferring to nearby hospitals or clinics, depending on the severity of the injuries or illnesses.

School Health Center - Zhi Shan B1  >

Clinics

Off-campus medical clinics are within walking distance or reachable by bus. Students must bring their health insurance card when seeking medical attention; only a registration fee will be charged. If a student does not have their health insurance card, they will have to pay for the medical expenses themselves (which are more expensive)
