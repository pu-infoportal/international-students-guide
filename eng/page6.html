Tourist Spots

高美濕地 Gaomei Wetlands

Located in the Qingshui District of Taichung City, Gaomei Wetlands is home to various species including birds, fish, crabs, and other invertebrates. The wetland is most famous for its stunning sunset views, attracting many visitors who appreciate beautiful scenery. Each year in the autumn and winter, a large number of migratory birds arrive, making it a popular spot for birdwatchers.

Transportation Information
(from 'Providence University Station'):
Take bus 309 directly to the destination.

梧棲漁港
Wuchi Fishing Harbour

Located in the Wuchi District of Taichung City, Wuchi Fishing Port is one of the key ports on Taiwan's western coast. It is a popular sightseeing spot, surrounded by a market where visitors can buy fresh seafood, including various fish, crabs, and shellfish. Numerous restaurants, cafés, and snack stalls near the port offer delightful dining experiences. In the evening, you can witness stunning sunset views, making it a great place to explore.

Transportation Information
(from 'Providence University Station'):
Take bus 307 straight to 'Wuchi Tourist Fishing Port Station.'

東海大學路思義堂
Luce Memorial Chapel

The Luce Memorial Chapel at Tunghai University is located within the university campus and was built in 1963. It is designated as a national historical monument in Taiwan. The chapel features a roof and exterior walls made up of four curved surfaces covered with yellow glass tiles, intentionally allowing natural light to filter through the skylights, creating a mystical atmosphere.

彩虹眷村 Rainbow Village

Located in the Chun'an District of Nantun District, Taichung City, Rainbow Village is close to Ling Tung University. The 97-year-old Rainbow Grandpa, Mr. Huang Yongfu, who lives here, took up painting to create a colorful fairy tale paradise and leave a lasting memory. For tourists, taking photos to capture beautiful memories is the most important activity.

台中國家歌劇院
National Taichung Theater

The National Taichung Theater is situated in the Xitun District of Taichung City, close to the Taichung City Government. Designed by Japanese architect Toyo Ito in 2015, the building embodies the concept of a 'beautiful sound tunnel.' Its exterior is shaped like a wine bottle, showcasing a distinctive and elegant form. The National Taichung Theater serves not only as a venue for performing arts but also features numerous facilities for visitors, including cafés, an aerial garden, and shops, offering tourists a richer living experience and a place for knowledge exchange.

Transportation Information
(from 'Providence University Station'):
Take buses 300 to 310, get off at 'Shin Kong Mitsukoshi Station.' Then, walk approximately 5 minutes.

國立自然科學博物館
National Museum of Natural Science

The National Museum of Natural Science focuses on the collection and research of natural specimens and anthropological artifacts. The main exhibition area consists of six comprehensive sections, including the Science Center, Life Science Hall, Human Culture Hall, Earth Environment Hall, Botanical Garden, Outdoor Garden, and the museum theater.

台中公園 Taichung Park

Taichung Park is an interesting tourist destination in Taichung City, conveniently located just 700 meters from Taichung Train Station. The park features beautiful landscapes such as an artificial lake, pavilions, and curved bridges, making it not only a space for local residents to relax and unwind but also a popular spot for families, young people, and tourists. It has become a favored location for taking photos in Taichung.

Miyahara in Taichung is a famous Japanese dessert shop, known for its innovative dessert creations and premium ingredients. Nigh Markets in Taichung

Night markets are one of Taiwan's most iconic tourist attractions and a unique cultural phenomenon. Taichung City has many night markets, with stalls typically opening around 4 PM, offering a wide variety of food, games, and entertainment. People can taste traditional or innovative Taiwanese snacks and participate in various fun games and activities. Night markets are bustling, lively, and vibrant places that attract both locals and tourists. You can also experience the unique charm of Taiwan's night markets!

• 逢甲夜市 - Fengchia Night Market

• 一中街 - Yizhong Street Night Market

• 東海夜市 - Tunghai Night Market

• 靜宜夜市 - Jing Yi Night Market
