Transportation in Taichung

公車

When riding the BRT, the bus will stop at every station along Taiwan Boulevard. To get off at non-BRT stops, you need to press the stop button. You must tap your transportation card to get on and off the bus.

MRT
(Mass Rapid Transit)

The Green Line is currently operational, making it easy for commuting and exploring the city. 

Taichung Double Ten Bus Discount

Students enrolled in universities and other educational institutions in Taichung can enjoy this discount by registering a transportation card. This offer provides free travel for the first 10km and a maximum charge of 10 NTD for distances beyond that. There are 94 locations across Taichung where you can apply for it.

Providence University to Taichung Train Station

From Providence University, take the BRT buses 300 to 310 directly to Taichung Station (journey takes about 50 minutes). 

Providence University to HSR Taichung Station

From Providence University, take the BRT buses 300 to 310, get of at ‘City Hall Station'. Then, transfer to the MRT to go directly to 'Taichung High-Speed Rail Station.'
